Percentage Calculator

This Percentage Calculator helps you quickly find percentage values, percentage increase or decrease, and percentage share between numbers without needing to remember any formulas.

Use it to work out discounts, markups, results in exams, profit margins, or any situation where you need to compare one value against another in percentage terms.

What Can You Do With a Percentage Calculator?

A percentage calculator lets you solve common percentage questions such as “what is X% of Y”, “what percentage is A of B”, “how much has a value increased or decreased over time”, and “what is the discounted price after a certain percentage off”. It removes the need for manual calculations and reduces the chances of basic arithmetic mistakes.

When Is a Percentage Calculator Useful?

This calculator is useful when you are checking discounts while shopping, reviewing exam scores, analysing profit or loss, or comparing performance metrics. Instead of setting up separate formulas each time, you can enter the numbers and get a ready result in seconds.

Which Inputs Are Needed for Percentage Calculations?

Percentage calculations usually need two main values: a base value and a comparison value. Depending on what you are solving, you may enter a percentage and a total, a starting value and a final value, or two numbers you want to compare as a percentage of each other.

How Does This Percentage Calculator Help in Daily Decisions?

This percentage calculator helps you make quicker, more confident decisions in everyday situations. By seeing clear, accurate percentage results, you can better evaluate offers, track progress, and communicate numbers in a format that is easy for everyone to understand.
