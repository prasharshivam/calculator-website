Markup vs Margin for Small Business

Many small business owners confuse markup and profit margin. A 20% markup on cost is not the same as a 20% margin on selling price, and using the wrong one can lead to under‑pricing or over‑pricing products. The markup vs margin tab lets you enter cost and a percentage and then shows the matching selling price, markup and margin values side by side.

This makes it easier to set prices that cover costs, hit your target profit percentage and still stay competitive in your market.
