SIP Calculator Formula Explained

Uses XIRR formula: FV = P × (((1 + r)^n - 1) / r) × (1 + r) where P=monthly SIP, r=monthly return rate, n=months. Inflation adjustment: Real Value = Nominal / (1 + inflation/100)^years

Why Inflation Matters in SIP Planning

At 6% inflation, ₹1 crore after 20 years = ₹31.2 lakh real value today. Most calculators ignore this - ours shows both so you plan for actual lifestyle costs.

Step-Up SIP vs Regular SIP Comparison

₹5,000 monthly regular SIP @12% for 15 years = ₹20.2 lakh. Same with 10% yearly step-up = ₹41.8 lakh. Salary growth = investment growth.
