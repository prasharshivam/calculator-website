Financial Calculators

Use these financial calculators to plan EMIs, SIPs, mortgages, salary payouts and long‑term investments without opening a spreadsheet. Each tool is built to be fast, distraction‑free and easy to understand so you can focus on your money decisions.

Whether you are comparing home loan offers, estimating retirement savings or checking if an investment is beating inflation, these calculators give you a clear numeric picture in seconds.

Choose a Financial Calculator

  • SIP Calculator – estimate how monthly mutual fund investments can grow over time with compounding.
  • Simple Interest Calculator – quickly compute interest on loans or deposits that use a flat rate.
  • Compound Interest Calculator – see how reinvested interest accelerates growth for long‑term goals.
  • Mortgage / Home Loan Calculator – check EMIs, total interest and payoff schedule for housing loans.
  • ROI Calculator – measure percentage return on any investment after costs or fees.
  • Salary Calculator – convert CTC to in‑hand salary and understand common deductions.
  • 401k / Retirement Calculator – project how regular retirement contributions might grow until you stop working.

How These Financial Calculators Help You Decide

Good financial decisions need numbers, not guesses. These calculators highlight EMIs, interest costs, maturity values and returns so you can compare options like rent versus buy, SIP versus lump sum, or one loan offer against another.

Because the tools are free and anonymous, you can try multiple scenarios—changing interest rates, tenures or contribution amounts—until you find a plan that fits both your monthly budget and long‑term goals.
