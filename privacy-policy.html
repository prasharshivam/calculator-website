Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected and used when you visit yoursimplecalculator.com (“Your Simple Calculator”, “the website”, “we”, or “us”). The site offers free online calculators for finance, health, math and everyday tasks, and is intended for general information purposes only.

By using the website, you agree that your visit may be subject to the practices described in this Privacy Policy and any updates that are published on this page.

Information You Enter in Calculators

The calculators on this website work with the numbers you type into the input fields, such as loan amounts, interest rates, salaries, dates or health‑related values. These values are processed in your browser to show the result and are not intended to identify you personally.

You should avoid entering names, account numbers, or any sensitive personal details into the calculators because they are not required for the tools to function.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Your Simple Calculator may use cookies and similar technologies to keep the website running smoothly, remember basic preferences and understand how visitors interact with different pages. Cookies are small files stored on your device that can help recognise your browser over time.

You can control or delete cookies through the settings of your browser. If you choose to block certain cookies, some parts of the website may not work exactly as intended, but the core calculator functions will usually remain available.

Advertising and Google AdSense

To keep the calculators free to use, this website may display third‑party advertisements, including ads served by Google AdSense and other advertising partners. These partners may use cookies and similar technologies to show ads that are relevant to your interests, measure ad performance and prevent misuse of their services.

Google and its partners may use advertising cookies so that ads can be served based on your visits to this and other sites on the internet. You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites and apps that use its services, and how to manage your ad settings or opt out of personalised advertising, by visiting Google’s dedicated policy and account pages.

Analytics and Usage Data

The website may use privacy‑respecting analytics tools to understand general traffic patterns, such as which pages are visited most frequently, the type of device and browser used, and approximate geographic region. This information is used in aggregated form to improve the layout, content and performance of the calculators.

Analytics data is not intended to be used to identify individual visitors. It is reviewed in summary form to help decide which calculators to improve or add in the future.

Links to Other Websites

Some pages may contain links to external websites or services that are not operated by Your Simple Calculator. If you click these links, you will be directed to a third‑party site with its own privacy and cookie policies.

We are not responsible for the content, security or privacy practices of third‑party websites, and you are encouraged to review their policies before providing any information or using their services.

Children’s Privacy

Your Simple Calculator is intended for general use and is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. The site does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that information about a child has been provided inappropriately, you can contact us so that the matter can be reviewed.

Information Security

Reasonable measures are taken to keep the website available and to reduce the risk of unauthorised access or misuse. However, no website or internet transmission can be completely secure, and you should always be cautious when sharing information online.

Your Choices and Controls

You can choose to limit cookies through your browser settings and may clear existing cookies at any time. You may also use tools provided by major browsers and advertising networks to adjust your advertising preferences or to opt out of certain types of tracking.

If you no longer wish to use the website, you can simply close the page or stop visiting the site. The calculators do not require registration or a user account.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in the website, applicable regulations or advertising and analytics practices. The “last updated” date at the bottom of this page indicates when the policy was most recently revised.

Continued use of the website after changes are posted will be treated as acceptance of the updated policy.
