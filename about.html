About Your Simple Calculator

Your Simple Calculator is a small, independent project created to make everyday calculations faster and less confusing. The website brings together focused tools for finance, health, math, and daily planning so that you can get clear numbers without downloading apps or signing up for an account.

Each calculator is designed to be straightforward: you enter a few values, get an instant result, and can adjust the inputs as many times as you like. The goal is to remove jargon and present calculations in a format that most people can understand at a glance.

What You Can Do Here

Your Simple Calculator includes tools for tasks such as checking loan payments, estimating savings growth, reviewing salary and tax scenarios, monitoring health metrics, and solving basic math questions. New calculators are added over time based on questions that people frequently search for and topics that are useful in everyday life.

Results from the calculators are intended for general information and planning. They are not professional financial, medical, or legal advice, and important decisions should always be discussed with a qualified expert or service provider.

How the Website Is Supported

To keep the calculators free to use, Your Simple Calculator may display third‑party advertisements and use basic analytics to understand how the site is used. Advertising partners and analytics providers may use cookies or similar technologies to show relevant ads and measure performance in line with their own policies.

You can learn more about how information is handled, which cookies may be used, and how ads are served by reading the dedicated Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions pages linked in the footer of the site.

Contact and Feedback

Your Simple Calculator is continually being improved. If you notice an error in a formula, have a suggestion for a new calculator, or would like to report a technical issue, you can reach out using the contact details provided on the Contact or Privacy Policy page.

Feedback from users helps identify new features, improve explanations on each calculator page, and keep existing tools more accurate and useful.
